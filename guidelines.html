Our services

Guidelines


Step 1: Access the Web

Users access the website: medisure.org to experience the best products and use the latest service features.

Step 2: Register A New Account

Enter persional information to register a new account via Sign up link, such as:

  • Username
  • Email
  • Choose Gender
  • Password
  • Confirm Password
  • Select Avatar(If any)

    •   Pressing Register Button to complete account creation.

    Step 3: Login to the system

    Log in to the system to be able to schedule an appointment via Login link
    By Entering: Username and Password
      Pressing Login Button to login to the system.

    Step 4: Creating Patient Record

     Option 1: If you have been to the hospital, go to the medical record to update the condition of your body or symptoms and select the Specialist you want to examine.

     Option 2: Never examined, register a new patient record with some information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Select Specialty
  • Describe medical condition or symptoms

    •   Pressing Save Button to completely create patient record.

    Step 5: Making An Appointment

    Choose a doctor (Search doctors by specialty or filter by gender) and Choose Time

      Pressing Book Button to make an appointment

    Step 6: Waiting the doctor confirmed the appointment

    After the patient makes an appointment, Customer Service will confirm the appointment via Email or Call to confirm.

    Step 7: Precausions And Performance

    >> On the day of the examination, the patient should go directly to the clinic or the reception counter according to the instructions on the examination card.
    >> The patient are examined according to hospital procedures.
    >> The patient returns to the original clinic, after receiving the full examination results from the doctor including consultation, prescription and notes.

    Step 8: Payment

    Check payment information (medical fee, utility fee and total amount) and Confirm payment.
