  • General issue

  • “What are the benefits of using this online medical registration software?"

    Book an appointment by appointment, anytime, anywhere, without going to the hospital
      No waiting in line to get a medical check-in number d
     Reduce waiting time at the hospital.
     Receive information of electronic medical examination form through software
     Actively choose the examination schedule (date of examination, examination time frame, examination doctor)
     Reminder to schedule a follow-up appointment, book an automatic follow-up appointment
     Look up medical examination results online through software.
     Easily access and receive new notifications and information from the hospital

  • "How do I sign up?"

    The software can be accessed and used on all devices that can access the internet. (3G, 4G, 5G, Wifi, network wire ...)
    Desktop, laptop: visit website
    Tablets and other devices……
    Provide personal information as: username, password, email,...

  • "Is there a fee to register for online medical examination?"

    When using the online medical examination registration service through the software, there is no charge and the use of other features is completely free.

  • "Does the software support medical registration 24/7?"

    The Software allows you to register for medical examination at any time of the day and any day of the week, ensuring that you can use the Software to register for medical examination anytime, anywhere, without having to Go directly to the hospital to do it.

  • "After successful registration, how will I receive the medical examination card?"

    You will receive an electronic medical record directly on the software. Management section “My Account”.

  • "How do I know that the payment was successful?"

    After examining and receiving medicine, the manager will check the paid patient and that appointment on the user's account will display more details and see the doctor's note.

  • "After successfully registering for medical examination through the software, is it possible to cancel the appointment?"

    After successful registration, if the patient wants to cancel the appointment, he can delete the appointment 1 day in advance, but if the patient does not come to this appointment, the appointment will be automatically canceled.

  • "I arrived at the hospital later than scheduled, will I be examined or not?"

    In case you are late compared to the appointment time on the medical examination card, you can still go to the hospital to be examined, but all reception and examination time will be according to the hospital's arrangement, depending on the actual situation. hospital and clinic at that time.
